# Justin Welsh - Content Systems for Solopreneurs

Video Topic: Building an Audience with Consistent Content

## Key Takeaways

- Publish consistently.
- Develop repeatable writing systems.
- Repurpose one idea into multiple pieces of content.
- Focus on helping a niche audience.
- Measure long-term growth instead of short-term engagement.

## Why It Matters

Justin Welsh demonstrates how creators and founders can build authority through simple, repeatable content systems.
