# Anthony Pierri

## Topic
B2B messaging and positioning.

## Key Takeaways

- Clear messaging beats clever messaging.
- Explain the customer's problem before presenting the solution.
- Focus on business outcomes instead of product features.
- Use customer language in marketing copy.
- Keep positioning simple and memorable.

## Why It Matters

Strong messaging helps B2B buyers quickly understand the value of a product.
