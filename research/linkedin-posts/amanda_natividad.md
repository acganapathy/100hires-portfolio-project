## Topic
Audience-first content marketing for B2B SaaS.

## Key Takeaways

- Create content for a specific audience instead of everyone.
- Solve real customer problems with every post.
- Use simple language and avoid unnecessary jargon.
- Build trust through consistency and helpful insights.
- Repurpose high-performing content across different platforms.

## Why It Matters

Amanda focuses on creating content that attracts and educates the right audience instead of chasing algorithms.
